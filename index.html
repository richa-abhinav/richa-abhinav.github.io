First Meet Each Other

Our first meeting felt like fate itself weaving its magic. That day, as Abhinav found himself in conversation with Richa’s friend, Richa appeared as if summoned by destiny. She often calls it fate, but only Abhinav knows how many moments he had secretly hoped for a chance to speak with her. The universe conspired in the smallest ways—Richa’s friend missed her bus, and Richa returned, unknowingly stepping into a new chapter. By the time she arrived, her friend was already engaged in laughter with Abhinav. And there, among chance and serendipity, Richa met Abhinav for the very first time. Neither of them could imagine the beautiful journey that awaited them.

Richa's Words for Abhinav...

Courage, to me, means the ability to face fear, difficulties, and the uncertainty to give up when life tries so hard to break you. And when I think about courage, all I can see is "YOU".You may not realize that you have the ability to keep smiling even after the unbearable problems that have come across you. This life has given you so many problems, heartbreak, etc., but with it, you have become this version where anyone can rely on you. You don't even(cause you are stupid) know that you have a smile just like the first rain after the long summer - refreshing, gentle, and full of hope. Just like a child who’s scared or hurting cries out “MAMA,” people call out to you when their world feels heavy. And why? Because somewhere along the way, without even trying, you became that kind of presence in their lives. You care for people as if their hearts are treasures. You make them feel seen, valued, and important. You listen when their voices shake. You stay even when they try to push you away. You don’t disappear when things get dark—you hold your ground beside them. And that’s why, in their moments of fear or confusion, the first person they think of is you. Because to them, you are safe. You are home. Well, you’ve become someone’s safest place. But now I want to know—where is your safest place? You told me yesterday that I am your best friend. From now on, tell me everything. Even if I can’t fix it, I will listen. And I will try my best to help you find a way through it. Let me tell you this: I’m a good listener. I want you to show the emotions you’ve buried so deep inside yourself. I want you to let them out. I want you to know that you can cry in front of me—because men can cry, and it’s okay not to be okay. So, as the youngest son of the house, Mr. Abhinav Sharma…can I, Richa Gusain, the eldest daughter of the house, become your safest place? A place where you can share everything— your thoughts, your fears, your worries, your heart.
